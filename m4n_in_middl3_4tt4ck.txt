Imagine you’re in a crowded café, sipping your coffee, connected to the free Wi-Fi. You feel safe, right? You’re just browsing, maybe checking your bank account, messaging a friend. But… what if I told you that someone else was reading everything you sent? Not just reading it—modifying it, manipulating it, changing reality itself?

This is the unsettling world of Man-in-the-Middle (MitM) attacks.
